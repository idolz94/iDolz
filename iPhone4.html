Iphone 4S

The Most Amazing Iphone Yet!

Iphone 4S, My first and favorite iPhone I have owned.

History :

  • May 2014 - some leaks had a fairly accurate description of the iPhone 4S including the name "iPhone 4S", the Apple A5 chip, new camera
  • October 4, 2011 - The iPhone 4S was unveiled at Apple's "Let's Talk iPhone" event, on the Apple Campus in Cupertino, California.

Software

  • Operating System - Original iOS 5.0
  • Best OS For 4S - iOS 7.1.2
  • Addition the most important software applications to debut on the 4S include :
  • - Siri
  • - iCloud
  • - iMessage
  • - Notification Center
  • - AirPlay
  • - Cards App
  • - iOS 5 support

Hardware

  • Chipset - Apple A5 processor
  • CPU - New dual-core 1.0 GHz Cortex-A9
  • GPU - PowerVR SGX543MP2
  • Camera - 8MP f/2.4, 35mm, LED flash
  • Video - Recording 1080p@30fps

Memory

  • Card slot - No
  • Internel Memory - 8/16/32/64 GB
  • Ram - 512MB

Battery

  • Internet - 6 hours 3G user
